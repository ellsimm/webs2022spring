A Few Words About This Site

This website was created as an assignment for The University of Pittsburgh’s Composing Digital Media Class taught by Benjamin Miller (thanks Ben for this assignment!). We were tasked with creating a site about absolutely anything, and the goal was to demonstrate our grasp of html and css in web design.

This site was created at a time in my life when, you guessed it, I was crying a lot. My original idea was simply a site ranking all of the places I have cried while attending Pitt. However, as I added the content, I realized that the nature of the site begged for a bit more. As you are viewing it today, it became a slightly satire piece on my own experience with depression and emotion, including mental health resources and the science behind the benefits of crying.

I hope this site can garner a laugh or two, and maybe even point out a resource for someone in need. The whole point of Crying @ Pitt is to let viewers know that they’re not alone, and it gets better. We’re all in this together; so keep on crying.
