Life is hard, but there is one scientifically proven way to make it at least a little better: crying. This site is dedicated to giving you tips on where, how, and why to cry. While some of this page is hopefully at least a little funny, mental health is no joke. So before we dive in...

... a few reminders:

  • You are not alone! But if you feel that way right now, that's ok too. Our resources page has a few ideas on how to get started on looking for help and support
  • It gets better. I swear. I promise. I know it might sound dumb, but it does.
  • Crying is arguably great, but crying too much (excessively, uncontrallably) might mean its time to talk to a professional. Here is the article I read when I first started looking for help. It can be daunting, but oh boy is it worth it.
  • Just another bullet that says You are not alone Think of it this way: you are on a website that someone made solely based on that fact that they were crying so much that they felt they had enough data to tell you the best places to cry.
  • Please be aware of triggers on this site. There is discussion of mental illness and briefly alcohol.
